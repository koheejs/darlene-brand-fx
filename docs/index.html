Good service starts with knowing your customer.

Scaling & retaining customers while still tailoring to their personal preferences makes you indispensable. This is a pivotal time for brands & retailers to connect with consumers in new digital ways as they redevelop their habits and decision journeys which are never linear. The pandemic catalyzed the evolution to digital behaviour, but the reality remains that over 70% of marketing and commerce is done offline, through traditional channels.

Traditional retail has its own distinctive traits which provide opportunities for innovation, and should be complementary to online retail, by finding ways to exploit the reasons that make them unique. When it comes to building trust in your brand, we must capitalize on brick & mortar human connection to people and augment the personal experience.

Brand-FX is obsessively focused on empowering traditional brands and retailers with modern solutions to co-exist with online: that’s our white space.
